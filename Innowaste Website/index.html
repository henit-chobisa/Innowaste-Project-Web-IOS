ABOUT

We at InnoWaste won't let your waste go into the dump and pollute the environment. We take your waste from the comfort of your homes and give it to the recycling units. We dont mix your waste and we give the option of separating it into diffrent categories like plastic, textile, metal, paper, glass etc. We even give you an incentive to give your waste to us. We give you points in exchange of the waste that you provide us. These points can be utilised to access discount coupons and offers on our merchandise.

Services

We provide you with the service of collecting dry waste once the amount of your waste cross the threshold value.

Community

We provide you a platform where you can share your activites take part in the challenges and events hosted on our app.

Merchandise

We have our own store where you can access discounts and offers based on the points that you will earn by giving us your waste.
